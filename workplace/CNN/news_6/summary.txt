The Alzheimer's Association offers a 24/7 helpline for information and support on Alzheimer's and dementia, as well as resources on diagnosis, research, prevention, treatments, and caregiver support. They promote awareness, education, and early detection of Alzheimer's. Their comprehensive guide covers topics such as diagnosis, caregiving, planning for the future, clinical trials, care options, safety, financial planning, and resources for specific communities. The guide aims to help individuals with Alzheimer's navigate the challenges of the disease and provides support throughout their journey. Additionally, the organization provides information on research grants, clinical trials, professional researchers, and opportunities to get involved in research efforts.
