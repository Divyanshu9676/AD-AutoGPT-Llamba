The Alzheimer's Association offers a 24/7 helpline and comprehensive resources for individuals with Alzheimer's disease and their caregivers. They provide information on understanding the diseases, diagnosis, research, prevention, treatments, and support for caregivers. The organization is committed to research and providing assistance to those affected by Alzheimer's and dementia, including information on clinical trials, brain donation, and navigating treatment options. They also offer guidance on managing symptoms, caregiving, safety, legal and financial planning, communication strategies, and maintaining overall well-being. Additionally, they provide information on research grants, professional researchers, and opportunities to participate in studies and research efforts.
