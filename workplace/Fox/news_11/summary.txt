The Alzheimer's Association offers a 24/7 helpline for support and information on Alzheimer's and dementia, providing resources on understanding the diseases, diagnosis, treatments, research, prevention, and support for caregivers. They also offer information on clinical trials, medications, and alternative treatments. The comprehensive guide covers topics such as diagnosis, daily care, treatment options, legal and financial planning, stages of the disease, care options, safety measures, and resources for support. It also includes information on research, brain health, community resources, the IDEAS Study, clinical trials, research grants, professional researchers, and partnerships related to Alzheimer's disease. The document encourages individuals to get involved in research and provides resources for finding clinical trials.
