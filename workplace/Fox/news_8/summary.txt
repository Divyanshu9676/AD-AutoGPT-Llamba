Alzheimer's Disease is a progressive brain disorder that affects memory, thinking, and behavior with no cure. Early diagnosis is important for managing symptoms. Treatment options include medications and therapies. Support and resources are available through organizations like alz.org. A comprehensive guide covers topics such as diagnosis, caregiving, safety, legal and financial planning, and community resources. Information on research, clinical trials, and resources for specific communities is also provided.
