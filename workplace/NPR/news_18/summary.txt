Alzheimer's disease is the leading cause of dementia in the UK, impacting memory and cognitive abilities. Risk factors include age, family history, depression, and lifestyle choices. Symptoms include memory loss, confusion, and personality changes. Diagnosis involves assessment by a GP and possible referral to specialists. While incurable, medications and support services can help manage symptoms. Lifestyle factors may reduce risk, and research is ongoing. Support and resources are available for individuals and caregivers affected by dementia.
