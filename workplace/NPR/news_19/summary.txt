The Alzheimer's Association offers a wide range of resources and support for individuals with Alzheimer's disease and their caregivers, including a 24/7 helpline, information on diagnosis and treatment, research updates, tips for memory concerns, and resources on clinical trials and prevention. They also provide information on managing symptoms, caregiving tips, safety measures, legal and financial planning, and community resources. Additionally, they offer information on research grants, professional researchers, and opportunities to participate in studies and research efforts.
