The Alzheimer's Association offers a 24/7 helpline for support and information on Alzheimer's and dementia, providing resources on understanding the diseases, diagnosis, treatments, research, prevention, and support for caregivers. They also offer information on clinical trials, brain donation, and navigating treatment options. The organization's comprehensive guide covers topics such as diagnosis, caregiving, safety, legal and financial planning, stages and behaviors of Alzheimer's, caregiver health, resources for specific communities, brain health tips, and research. It also provides information on research progress, clinical trials, prevention, brain donation, funding programs, and opportunities for involvement in research.
