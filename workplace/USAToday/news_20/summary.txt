Alzheimer's disease is caused by protein accumulation in the brain, leading to brain cell death. Lifestyle factors like exercise, diet, sleep, cognitive activities, and social connections can help prevent or slow its progression. Moderate alcohol intake, particularly wine, may have conflicting benefits. Recognizing symptoms early is important for early detection. Signing up for health alerts from Harvard Medical School can provide tips on various health topics and a free copy of the Best Diets for Cognitive Fitness.
